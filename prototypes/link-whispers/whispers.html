How it works:

  • Links have data attributes: data-preview-title, data-preview-desc, data-preview-type
  • On hover with 500ms delay, preview card slides in
  • Card disappears when mouse leaves link
  • Minimal design maintains site aesthetic
  • Can be disabled per-link by omitting data attributes
